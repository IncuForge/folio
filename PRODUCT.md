# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Family catering business owners and kitchen chefs. Managing event schedules, planning menus, calculating scaled ingredients, and tracking collections. Accessible via PC and mobile kitchen screens.

## Product Purpose
Digitize catering operations locally and securely. Main actions: taking custom orders, managing item libraries with seasonal availability, calculating scaled recipe sheets, printing menus/preps, and tracking payments.

## Brand Personality
Sophisticated, Minimalist, Editorial. Premium paper-restaurant menu aesthetic. Solid black ink, warm white paper, charcoal tones, clean rules. Zero fluff.

## Anti-references
AI-slop SaaS dashboards, rounded card grids, glassmorphism shadows, neon gradients, side-stripe alert borders, generic uppercase kickers.

## Design Principles
1. **Paper Restraint**: Approach the UI like high-quality stationery. Rely on solid borders, precise margins, and white space rather than boxes, shadows, and backgrounds.
2. **Signal Sparingly**: Maintain a monochromatic canvas. Reserve color strictly for high-priority alerts and payment status milestones to capture immediate focus.
3. **Typography as Structure**: Set visual hierarchy through a single, well-tuned font family (Inter) using weight, size, tracking, and line height.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high text contrast (>= 4.5:1) against the light background. Respect prefers-reduced-motion settings. Provide touch-friendly tap targets on mobile screen sizes.
